                                  THE ENERGY ENCYCLOPEDIA

Energy exists in many different forms. Examples of these are: light energy, heat energy, mechanical energy, gravitational energy, electrical energy, sound energy, chemical energy, nuclear or atomic energy and so on. Each form can be converted or changed into the other forms. Although there are many specific types of energy, the three major forms are:


KINETIC ENERGY POTENTIAL ENERGY MECHANICAL ENERGY
